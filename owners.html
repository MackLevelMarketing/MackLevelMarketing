For Property Owners & Landlords

Longer Lease Terms. Reliable Rent. Zero Guest Management.

Mack Multi-Level Marketing LLC leases qualified properties directly from owners and professionally operates them as short-term rentals. You receive agreed monthly rental income while we handle the furnishing, cleaning, guest communication, upkeep, and daily operations.

A hands-off leasing model built for owner peace of mind.

This model is designed for owners who want stable rental income without managing tenants, guests, cleaning schedules, booking platforms, or hospitality operations.

100% On-Time Rent Focus

Our priority is simple: pay rent on time, every month, according to the lease terms. Owners should not have to chase payments.

Longer Lease Terms

We look for longer-term lease agreements that reduce vacancy concerns and create predictable rental income for property owners.

Professional Cleaning

Properties are cleaned regularly between guest stays by turnover cleaners, helping maintain presentation, comfort, and property standards.

Ongoing Upkeep

Because guest experience matters, we monitor the property closely and address basic upkeep needs quickly and professionally.

Simple owner benefit: You lease the property to us. We pay rent. We operate the short-term rental business. You stay hands-off.

How we protect the property experience.

Short-term rental operations require structure. Our model focuses on responsible guest expectations, clean presentation, and systems that help reduce owner stress.

Noise Monitoring

Where appropriate, we can use noise monitoring sensors to help discourage parties, excessive noise, and unwanted disruptions.

Guest Standards

We set clear house rules, booking expectations, and guest guidelines to support respectful property use.

Turnover Checks

Frequent cleanings and turnovers create regular eyes on the property, helping identify issues earlier than a traditional long-term rental may.

Professional Presentation

We keep the property guest-ready, organized, and aligned with a higher hospitality standard.

What owners are not responsible for.

We are not asking you to become an Airbnb host or manage a hospitality business. The goal is to keep the owner role simple and clear.

No Guest Messaging

You do not answer guest questions, check-in messages, late-night requests, or booking inquiries.

No Cleaning Coordination

We coordinate cleanings, turnovers, restocking, and guest-ready preparation.

No Listing Management

We handle listing strategy, guest flow, calendar management, and short-term rental operations.

No Daily Involvement

Your role is the property owner. Our role is the operator. The experience is designed to be hands-off for you.

How the process works

01 · Property Qualification We review the property, location, layout, rules, lease restrictions, HOA requirements, and short-term rental suitability.
02 · Lease Terms Discussion We discuss rent amount, lease length, operating permissions, owner expectations, access, and maintenance responsibilities.
03 · Agreement & Setup Once terms are agreed, we sign the lease, prepare the property, furnish if needed, and create a professional guest-ready setup.
04 · Professional Operations We manage guests, cleanings, turnovers, upkeep, listing performance, house rules, and daily short-term rental operations.

Clear lease. Clear rent. Clear responsibilities.

Our goal is to create a professional arrangement where the owner receives consistent lease-based income and our company handles the short-term rental operation.
