Corporate Leasing Model for Property Owners

Mack Multi-Level Marketing LLC leases residential properties under structured long-term agreements, providing consistent rental income while managing all short-term rental operations.

What We Offer

We operate under a lease-based model. You remain the property owner while our company becomes the tenant and operator, handling all aspects of property use.

Consistent Rent Payments

Monthly rent paid according to lease terms with a focus on reliability and on-time payment.

Long-Term Lease Agreements

Structured lease terms that provide stability and reduce vacancy concerns.

Professional Cleaning & Upkeep

Regular cleaning between guest stays and ongoing attention to property condition.

Noise Monitoring Systems

Optional monitoring systems used to discourage parties and protect the property environment.

Owner Position: You collect rent.
Our Role: We manage operations.

How the Lease Structure Works

1. Property review and qualification

2. Lease terms discussion (rent, duration, expectations)

3. Lease agreement execution

4. Property setup and furnishing (if needed)

5. Full operational management by our company

Owner Responsibilities

Minimal Involvement

No guest communication or booking management required.

No Operational Duties

No cleaning coordination, check-ins, or listing management.

Standard Property Ownership

You maintain ownership while we operate under lease terms.
